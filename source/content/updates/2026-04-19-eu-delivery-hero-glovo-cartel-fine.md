## EC Fines Delivery Hero and Glovo €329M for Food Delivery Cartel

- **Date**: 2026-04-19
- **Region**: EU
- **Company relevance**: **Delivery Hero** — Naspers/Prosus portfolio company
- **Priority**: CRITICAL
- **Source**: [PYMNTS / European Commission](https://www.pymnts.com/cpi-posts/european-commission-launches-antitrust-investigation-into-delivery-hero-and-glovo/)

On 2 June 2025, the European Commission fined Delivery Hero and Glovo a combined €329 million for operating an online food delivery cartel. The Commission found that the two companies — while Delivery Hero was also Glovo's largest shareholder — coordinated their conduct by sharing commercially sensitive information and agreeing not to compete in each other's territories. This constitutes a serious breach of Article 101 TFEU, the EU's prohibition on anti-competitive agreements. For Prosus, which holds a stake in Delivery Hero via its Naspers lineage, this is a direct portfolio-level regulatory incident. The fine is material to Delivery Hero's financials and signals that the Commission will apply cartel rules firmly even where a company holds a minority ownership position in a competitor. The case may also prompt renewed scrutiny of other portfolio companies' coordination practices where they operate in overlapping markets.
